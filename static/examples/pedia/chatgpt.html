How it works

A transformer-based large language model trained on text from the internet, then fine-tuned with reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) to follow instructions and refuse harmful requests.

Impact

Triggered a wave of generative-AI products and a global race among Microsoft, Google, Meta, and Anthropic. Reshaped how millions of people draft, code, and learn.

Concerns

Hallucinations, copyright lawsuits, classroom misuse, election disinformation, and the labour-market impact on knowledge work all remain active debates.
