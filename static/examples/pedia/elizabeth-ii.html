Elizabeth II

Queen of the United Kingdom and 14 other Commonwealth realms from her accession on 6 February 1952 until her death on 8 September 2022 — a reign of 70 years and 214 days, the longest of any British monarch.

A reign in moments

  1. 1947
    Marries Lieutenant Philip Mountbatten at Westminster Abbey.
  2. 1953
    Coronation at Westminster Abbey, watched on TV by 27M Britons.
  3. 1969
    Royal Family documentary opens the palace doors.
  4. 1981
    Wedding of Charles & Diana — 750M global viewers.
  5. 1992
    "Annus horribilis" — Windsor Castle fire and royal divorces.
  6. 2002
    Golden Jubilee marking 50 years on the throne.
  7. 2012
    Diamond Jubilee & iconic Olympic opening with 007.
  8. 2022
    Platinum Jubilee in June; death at Balmoral on 8 September.
