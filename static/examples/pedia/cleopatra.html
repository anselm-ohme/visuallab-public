CLEOPATRA VII

Last active ruler of the Ptolemaic Kingdom of Egypt (51-30 BC). A multilingual scholar and shrewd diplomat who allied with Julius Caesar and Mark Antony — and whose death ended 3,000 years of pharaonic rule.

A 21-year reign

  1. 51 BC
    Co-ruler of Egypt with her younger brother Ptolemy XIII at age 18.
  2. 48 BC
    Smuggled into Caesar's quarters reportedly inside a rolled carpet.
  3. 47 BC
    Bears Caesar's son Caesarion (Ptolemy XV).
  4. 44 BC
    Caesar assassinated; she returns to Alexandria.
  5. 41 BC
    Meets Mark Antony at Tarsus; political & personal alliance.
  6. 31 BC
    Defeated at the naval Battle of Actium by Octavian.
  7. 30 BC
    Antony & Cleopatra die; Egypt becomes a Roman province.
