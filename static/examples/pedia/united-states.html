A 250-year arc

1776 · Founding
Declaration of Independence; Revolutionary War follows.
1861 · Civil War
Secession of 11 states; Union victory ends slavery.
1945 · Superpower
WWII victory and the dawn of the nuclear age.
1969 · Apollo 11
Neil Armstrong becomes the first human on the Moon.
1991 · Cold War ends
Soviet collapse leaves the US as sole superpower.
2008 · Financial crisis
Triggers a global recession; reshapes regulation.
2020s · AI & reshaping
US firms lead the modern AI revolution.
Today
Largest economy, military, and cultural exporter.

Economy

High-income, technology-driven service economy. Reserve currency status of the US dollar underpins global finance.

Culture

Hollywood, Silicon Valley, and a vast university system make the US the world's largest exporter of cultural and digital products.

Diversity

A nation of immigrants — over 40 million foreign-born residents and the most spoken languages of any country.
