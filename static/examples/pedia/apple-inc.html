A 50-year arc

1976 · Apple I
Wozniak builds the first Apple computer; sold for $666.66.
1984 · Macintosh
Iconic Super Bowl ad; first mainstream GUI computer.
1997 · Jobs returns
Apple weeks from bankruptcy; "Think Different" campaign.
2001 · iPod
"1,000 songs in your pocket" — Apple becomes a consumer brand.
2007 · iPhone
"Today, Apple reinvents the phone." Smartphone era begins.
2018 · $1T cap
First US public company to cross a trillion dollars in value.
2020 · Apple Silicon
Macs leave Intel for in-house ARM-based M-series chips.
2024 · $3T & AI
Apple Intelligence ships; market cap holds above $3 trillion.

Founders

Steve Jobs (1955-2011), Steve Wozniak (b. 1950), and Ronald Wayne (b. 1934, sold his 10% stake for $800 just twelve days in).

Brand power

Consistently ranked #1 most valuable global brand by Interbrand and Kantar — worth an estimated $500B in brand equity alone.

Cupertino & the world

Headquartered at Apple Park ("the Spaceship") in Cupertino. ~165,000 employees worldwide and 530+ retail stores in 26 countries.
