A career arc

From the youngest Jackson 5 brother (1969) to global superstar (1980s) to billion-dollar This Is It comeback that ended with his death in June 2009.

Influence

A defining influence on Beyoncé, Bruno Mars, Justin Timberlake, The Weeknd and on K-pop's choreography-first model.

Controversies

Allegations of child sexual abuse — denied by Jackson and his estate — have remained a contested part of his legacy.
