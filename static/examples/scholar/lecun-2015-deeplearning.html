The deep-learning idea, in one image

Stack many layers of simple non-linear units. Each layer learns a more abstract representation of the input than the one below.

The three Turing laureates

  • Yann LeCun
    Convolutional networks (1989), Chief AI Scientist at Meta.
  • Yoshua Bengio
    Word embeddings, attention, MILA founder; safety advocate.
  • Geoffrey Hinton
    Backpropagation, Boltzmann machines, AlexNet co-author; 2024 Nobel in Physics.
  • 2018 Turing Award
    All three jointly received computing's highest prize for the deep-learning revolution.

Six big themes

  • 1 · Representation learning
    Models discover features instead of relying on hand-crafted ones.
  • 2 · Backprop & SGD
    Train deep nets end-to-end with cheap, scalable gradient descent.
  • 3 · Convolutional networks
    Local connectivity + weight sharing — the right prior for images.
  • 4 · Recurrent / LSTMs
    Sequence modelling for language, speech, time series.
  • 5 · Distributed representations
    Words and concepts as dense vectors enable composition.
  • 6 · The future
    Unsupervised pre-training, reinforcement learning, reasoning.
