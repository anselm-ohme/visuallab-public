Masked language modelling

Randomly mask 15% of tokens; train the model to predict the originals using the entire bidirectional context.

The capital of [MASK] is Paris.
model predicts
France
Bidirectional context — both "capital" before and "Paris" after — disambiguates the answer.

Two pre-training objectives

  • Masked Language Model
    15% of tokens replaced with [MASK]; predict the originals.
  • Next Sentence Prediction
    Given two sentences, predict if B follows A — for sentence-pair tasks.

Loss formulation

$$\mathcal{L} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{MLM}} + \mathcal{L}_{\text{NSP}}$$

Fine-tune one model, hit every task

Add a small task-specific head to the pre-trained BERT encoder, fine-tune for a few epochs.
