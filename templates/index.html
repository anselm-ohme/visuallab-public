Turn any academic paper or GitHub repository into a live, animated explainer.

VisualLab pulls academic papers from arXiv and Semantic Scholar, and repositories from GitHub, sends them to two NVIDIA-hosted LLMs running in parallel, and streams self-contained HTML dashboards back into your browser. Built for academics who want to understand research and codebases faster — or to present a paper, including their own, in a quick visual format.
